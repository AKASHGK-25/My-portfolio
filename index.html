About

I'm Akash G.K, a finance professional based in Chennai, currently working in US accounting and payroll compliance at Finstein Advizory Services, with a background built through statutory audit engagements at Sundaram & Srinivasan and freelance audit work with Grant Thornton and Protiviti on clients including Axis Bank, DBS Bank, Bhushan Steel and Lenskart.
